About

There is a little magic in finding something new and delicious - to learn what you like and share it with your family and friends. Food and shopping for my family is a big part of my life and I wanted to make it my job. I wanted to share the foods and provisions I discovered growing up in New York City and traveling the world for work and fun.

Community and shopping small is important to me. Living and working in Providence allows me to connect to with a vibrant community of shoppers, local producers and supplies from near and far.

At our small shop we offer unusual tasty pleasures from local businesses and providers from all over the world. We have a well curated selection so you can learn and try new things without having to leave Rhode Island.

Hawes has an array of seasonal items, everyday staples and gourmet classics so you can stop in to prepare for dinner, a picnic, a day on the boat or find unique gifts for your family and friends.
